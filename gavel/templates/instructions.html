Here's how to use Gavel.

Your first visit will require no action except to move on to the next project.

With each subsequent visit, you will be presented with another project to evaluate with respect to the previous project. Select the card with the better* project and click 'Continue' to move onto the next project.

If the project you are evaluating is inappropriate or suspicious as deemed by the judging criteria or rules of the event*, flag the project before moving on. Event staff will be alerted to manually review this project.

If the project you are being asked to evaluate is a no show or currently occupied by another judge, please select the corresponding option before moving on.

* Consult your event’s judging coordinator for criteria that makes one team “better” than another as well as what the event considers inappropriate, suspicious, cheating, or grounds for potential disqualification.

Before you continue, please acknowledge that you have read and understand the above instructions.
